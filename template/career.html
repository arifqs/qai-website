Get To Know Us

WHO WE ARE

We are a smart boutique SaaS company that is focused on disrupting old marketing and communication workflows for media companies around the world. Our customers are broadcasters, streaming services, film distributors, podcasting platforms and book publishers.

We are proud to work with some of the biggest brands in the entertainment industry and take pride in developing a product that solves the real problems that face customers when it comes to media/data sharing and distribution.

TRUE PARTNERSHIP

We describe our relationship with our customers as “a true partnership”. Since the beginning of Clipsource, we have been working with our customers to build a platform that truly meets their needs. We see the companies we work with as partners rather than customers.

When issues arise, we solve them in a timely manner where we do not disrupt our partner’s workflows and always try to adjust our solutions to their needs.

WORK CULTURE

Although we are founded way up north in Stockholm, Sweden, our team and customers are based all over the world. We pride ourselves on being a diverse international company that operates across languages, cultures and standards.

We believe that not only do you need to respect your teammates but you also need to be able to rely on them. At Clipsource everyone is willing to pitch in when they are needed and solve problems together, as a team. We work in agile teams, where sharing and learning are key elements and a natural part of our daily work.

GREAT TECHNOLOGY

A lot of software is built on hope, like, “yeah, we think this can be great.” At Clipsource, it is not just a vision or an idea, it is a real solution that is built with the people who use it 8 hours a day. We are proud to work closely with our customers and develop a software solution that is specifically designed to solve problems users face on a daily basis.

As the industry changes, we strive to continuously improve our product to meet the growing demands our customers have.
